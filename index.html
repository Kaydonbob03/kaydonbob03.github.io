Who am I?

Hi, I'm Kayden Cormier, a passionate software developer, full-stack enthusiast, and cybersecurity hobbyist. My journey into the world of technology began in high school, where I discovered my love for coding and problem-solving.

Currently, I'm in my second year of the Computer Programming program at Sault College, where I am honing my skills and expanding my knowledge in various domains of software development.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies, making changes to and debugging my own code, and making new programs and software from random and fun ideas I get. My diverse interests in technology drive me to continuously learn and innovate.
